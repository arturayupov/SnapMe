Privacy Policy for SnapMe – AI Photo Maker

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

SnapMe – AI Photo Maker (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides a mobile application that allows users to create AI-enhanced photos, portraits, and artwork from their images. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Images You Upload

To generate AI-enhanced photos, you may upload selfies or other images. These images are processed securely through our AI model and are temporarily stored only for the duration necessary to generate results. Images are automatically deleted after processing is complete.

1.2 Device Information

We may collect non-personal device data such as device model, operating system version, app version, and usage statistics for analytics and performance improvement.

1.3 App Activity

We collect anonymized information about how you use the app, including interaction events, feature usage, and crash logs. This helps us improve app stability and user experience.

1.4 Optional Email Contact

If you choose to contact our support team, we receive your email address and any information you voluntarily provide in your message.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-enhanced photos and artwork.
  • To improve app stability, performance, features, and user experience.
  • To provide customer support when requested.
  • To analyze usage trends for product improvement.

3. How We Store and Delete Your Data

3.1 Image Handling

  • Your uploaded images are used solely for generating results.
  • Images are not used to train our AI models.
  • Images are not shared with third parties.
  • Images are deleted automatically after processing is complete.

3.2 Data Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party tools for analytics, crash reporting, and performance improvement, including:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Google Play Services
  • Apple App Store Services

These services collect anonymized or aggregated data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

SnapMe – AI Photo Maker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.
