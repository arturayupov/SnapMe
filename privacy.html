Privacy Policy for SnapMe – AI Photo Maker

Effective Date: December 5, 2025

SnapMe – AI Photo Maker (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides a mobile application that allows users to create AI-enhanced photos, portraits, and artwork from their images. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Images You Upload (Face Data)

To generate AI-enhanced photos, you may upload selfies or other images. These images may contain face data. We collect and process this data solely to provide the AI generation features you request.

Specifics on Face Data:
  • We do not use this data to identify you or for authentication purposes.
  • We do not extract biometric identifiers (like face geometry or face prints) for storage.
  • Your original images are used only as a reference for the AI model to generate the stylized output.

1.2 Guest User Data

If you use the app without creating an account ("Guest Mode"), we assign a unique anonymous identifier to your device to track your daily usage limits (e.g., free generations). This identifier allows you to use the service without providing personal information like email or name.

1.3 Device Information

We may collect non-personal device data such as device model, operating system version, app version, and usage statistics for analytics and performance improvement.

1.4 App Activity

We collect anonymized information about how you use the app, including interaction events, feature usage, and crash logs. This helps us improve app stability and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-enhanced photos and artwork based on your uploaded reference images.
  • To manage your daily generation limits (for both registered and guest users).
  • To improve app stability, performance, features, and user experience.
  • To provide customer support when requested.

3. How We Store, Share, and Retain Your Data

3.1 Image Handling & Face Data Retention

  • Processing: Your uploaded images are processed securely through our cloud providers (Google Cloud/Firebase) solely for the generation task.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your face data or original images with third-party data brokers, marketing firms, or any other external parties not directly involved in the generation process.
  • Retention: Original images are stored in your private cloud storage within the app to allow you to reuse them for future generations. They are retained only as long as you maintain your account (or guest session).
  • Deletion: You can delete your images or your entire account at any time within the app settings, which will permanently remove your data from our servers.

3.2 Data Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses trusted third-party services for infrastructure and analytics:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure authentication, database storage, and cloud functions.
  • RevenueCat: Used to process in-app purchases and subscriptions.

These services process data securely and in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

SnapMe – AI Photo Maker is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
