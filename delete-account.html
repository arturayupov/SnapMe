Account & Data Deletion Request

This page provides instructions on how users of SnapMe – AI Photo Maker can request deletion of their account and all related personal data. This page is owned and maintained by the developer: Artur Aiupov.

How to Request Account Deletion

Step 1: Send a deletion request to our official support email:
Email: snapme.app.support@gmail.com
Step 2: Include the following information in the email:
  • The subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Your account email (if applicable)
  • The user ID displayed in the app (if available)
Step 3: Our team will process your request within 48–72 hours and notify you once the deletion is complete.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account information (email, OAuth profile, username)
  • All in-app history linked to your account
  • All stored preferences and settings

What Data Is Not Stored by SnapMe

SnapMe does not permanently store the following:

  • User uploaded images (selfies/photos)
  • AI-generated images
  • Any biometric or sensitive data

User images are sent to Google Gemini AI only for real-time processing and are deleted immediately after generation.

Data Retention Policy

  • Temporary user images: deleted instantly after processing
  • Account data: deleted within 48–72 hours of request
  • No data is shared with third parties except Google Gemini AI for processing
